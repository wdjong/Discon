Discon

The initial questions (is player 1 a person) determine how many players are participating in the game and what pieces they will use  to capture discs.

 

Pieces are moved in the same way a chess knight moves (an L); two squares in 1 direction and 1 square at right angles to that.

 

The help file (discon.hlp) contains the official rules as provided with the board game.

 

This document lists some helpful information relating to the computer version.

 

Because the board game can’t be purchased anymore and yet various members of my family have enjoyed and would like to continue to enjoy the game, it was decided to write a simple computer representation of it.

 

You move a piece by clicking on it and dragging it onto the disc you wish to include in your tower.

 

If you want to leave behind the tower and just move your piece you right-click and then drag (drag with the right button)

 

If you want to undo you can use Ctrl-z or click on the undo command.

 

Hovering over a piece tells you what height the tower is and what colour it is (assuming it’s a single colour)

 

Clicking on the tower briefly displays the component discs.
